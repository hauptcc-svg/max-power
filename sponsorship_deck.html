His Story

The Man
Behind the
Champion

In 2014, a medical procedure left Max partially paralysed. He was 22, living in one of Kariega's most under-resourced neighbourhoods, with no safety net and no clear path forward.

Four years later, he picked up a pair of two-kilogram dumbbells in his bedroom. Not in a gym. Not with a coach. Alone, with intention. He entered his first powerlifting competition in 2018 and won. He kept winning.

In 2021 he entered his first ever bodybuilding competition and was crowned South African National Champion — qualifying for the IBFF World Championships in Koper, Slovenia in the same moment. He won there too. And then again in 2023.

"I'm strong and able."
